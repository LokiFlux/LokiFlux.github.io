A1621

(Timelines)

Description

Seemingly graced with plenty of luck, this timeline has experienced a nearly century-long golden period of increasing prosperity, equality, and liberty, following a decisive victory of the Progressive Alliance in the great war against the Reds. Poverty, inequality, and all other infamous forms of discrimination have neary completely been eradicated, giving way to the development of a deeply solidaric society that focused on research and progress, redistributing the previously massive resource flow into war towards betterment of all of humanity.

Among many other achievements, this research uncovered the existence of parallel timelines, many of which less fortunate than A1621. Northstar was founded after this discovery and the decision to intervene in the fate of other timelines, using A1621's massive technological and societal progress and resources to aid those not yet blessed by the same cosmic fortune.
