DISCLAIMER

So it turns out I massively goofed and didn't think to check whether or not the names Unitarianism or Unitarian Party, which I used for my first antagonistic faction, were already in use IRL. Turns out, they are, for quite different groups with quite different beliefs. I sincerely apologize for the impression this may have created of me vilifying said groups which do exist in our world. This shouldn't have happened. I will be reworking this rather massive design flaw. For now, I've implemented a very plump, but functioning fix by simply renaming the faction and its members to "Antagonist" as a placeholder until I find a better name that *isn't* already in use IRL.

Scientists in a utopian world discover the existence of parallel timelines. Upon realising most of them have fallen into dystopia, Northstar is founded with the goal of helping those less fortunate.
