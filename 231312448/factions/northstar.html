Northstar

(Factions)

"All for one, one for all"

Description

Northstar is an inter-timeline task force dedicated to helping worlds from less fortunate timelines. Founded after physicists from timeline A1621 were the first to discover and document the existence of an infinite number of alternate timelines, quickly developing the technology to transfer information and matter across timelines, and making first contact with a transuniversal civilisation. Their numbers and resources were soon vastly expanded, as various civilisations gladly joined their ranks in a spirit of cooperation and solidarity. This exchange of knowledge, experience and culture signified a quantum leap in the march of progress.
