Background

Leon was born in 2005 in timeline B1627, under the tyrannical rule of the Unitarian Party. His childhood and adolescence were overshadowed by the experience of growing up as a transgender boy, as his mere existence made him one of the worst enemies of the state and society, according to Unitarian propaganda. Most would not have survived this ordeal. Most, in fact, did not. The public knowledge that most others either died by their own hand or in a disposal facility for the unwanted only fueled his rage and determination further.
After discovering a method of synthesising testosterone, starting a fully DIY transition, and consequently attempting to run away and live off the grid for a while, he was captured by the Unitarian government and due for public execution. Northstar politely disagreed with that decision and extracted him.

Psychological profile

Basic neuropsychological checkup uncovered partial dysfunction of amygdala, causing severely deficient fear response. Likely key reason behind abnormally high courage and resilience.
