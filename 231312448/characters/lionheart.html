Background

Leon was born in 2005 in timeline B1627, under the tyrannical rule of the Antagonist Party. His childhood and adolescence were overshadowed by the experience of growing up as a closeted transgender boy, as his mere existence made him one of the worst enemies of the state and society, according to Antagonist propaganda. Most would not have survived this ordeal. Most, in fact, did not. The public knowledge that most others either died by their own hand or, upon being outed, in a disposal facility for the unwanted, only fueled his rage and determination further.
After discovering a method of synthesising testosterone, testing it on himself, and being found out when the changes to his body became too visible to hide, he was captured by the Antagonist government and due for public execution. Northstar politely disagreed with that decision and extracted him.

Psychological profile

Basic neuropsychological checkup uncovered partial dysfunction of amygdala, causing severely deficient fear response. Likely key reason behind abnormally high courage and resilience. Borders on recklessness, however, may cause excessive and dangerous risk-taking behaviour. Deficient self-preservation instinct. Do not send into battle without backup and medical support.

Specialisation and skills

Above average intelligence, combined with highly advanced mastery of biochemistry. Underwent standard antagonist education career, choosing an apprenticeship in genetic engineering at the start of adolescence after completing his basic education. His skill quickly turned out to be at astonishing levels, seeing as he single handedly invented Hormone Replacement Therapy in his timeline and successfully initiated a transition completely by himself, a process that, in most known timelines where it is possible, requires the expertise of several professionals specialised in various fields of human medicine.
