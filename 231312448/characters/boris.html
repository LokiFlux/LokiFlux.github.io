Background

Psychological profile

Friendly, highly altruistic, and very protective of those he considers friends.

Specialisation and skills

Owing to his massive, muscular build and combat training, he is highly skilled at using heavy weaponry. He is capable of hip-firing an M2 Browning machine gun chambered in .50cal on full auto, something most others are uncapable of due to the M2's straight up violent recoil, which is why it's almost exclusively used as mounted artillery. Almost.
Even without brandishing a weapon, his raw strength and high weight allow him to knock out armored combatants or breach lightly reinforced doors with merely his bare hands. He is capable of lifting weights surpassing 500 kg for short periods of time, and carrying up to 200 kg over long distances.
Off the battlefield, his passion is vegan cuisine, preferrably cooking massive portions to cover either his daily nutrient needs, or to feed an entire battalion. He dislikes doing things in moderation in the kitchen as much as on the battlefield, opting to cook using flamethrowers, pots as large as himself, and going by the mantra "The more violently you cook, the better it tastes." He is rarely seen battling vegetables without loud music playing in the kitchen, preferrably death metal.
