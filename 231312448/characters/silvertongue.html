Background

Designated a gifted child in school, she was allowed to skip several classes and entering university at 16, going into law studies given her impressive memory and rhethorical skills, inspiring her desire to change the world from the speaker podium and court room.

Psychological profile

Possesses eidetic memory and impressive mental stamina. Owing to what appears to be a developmental defect of her brain's memory regulation, she is physically unable of forgetting anything.

Specialisation and skills

Her astonishing mental capabilities allow her to effortlessly memorize massive amounts of knowledge, which range from the majority of all laws of all states across all timelines known to her, to her speaking several hundreds of languages and dialects fluently, and even being able to learn a totally new language to a basic degree within mere hours.
